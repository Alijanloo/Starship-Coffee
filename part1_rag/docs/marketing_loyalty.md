# Customer Loyalty Program

Our loyalty program rewards frequent customers and encourages continued patronage through our points-based system integrated with crew identification.

## Points System

**Crew badge scans earn 2 points per credit.** This generous points system provides immediate value to our customers while encouraging regular visits to our caf√© locations.

## Points Redemption

Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases, free items, or special promotions. The flexible redemption system allows customers to choose how they want to use their accumulated points.

## Tier Benefits

Higher-tier customers receive additional benefits including priority service, exclusive menu items, and special event access. Our tier system recognizes and rewards our most loyal customers.

## Digital Integration

All points are tracked through our digital system, linked to crew identification badges. This seamless integration ensures accurate point tracking and easy redemption.

## Promotional Events

Special promotional events offer bonus points and exclusive rewards for loyalty program members. These events create excitement and encourage increased participation in our program.
